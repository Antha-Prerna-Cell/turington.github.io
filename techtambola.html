Tech Tambola

Intro

We must bring you to the event Tech Tambola. Here is an exciting event but with a technical TADKA!! Guess and strike correct answers instead of simple numbers. Pray your luck to be with you.

Rules:

  • Participants register individually for Tech Tambola.
  • There will be 30 quetions.
  • There will be 16 blocks in tambola slips.
  • Participants will get 30 seconds per question to solve it and for cross check the block.
  • Participants will be provided with tambola slip and a rough sheet.
  • No repetition of question in case someone misses a question or does not hear properly.
  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
  • There will be one winner and one runner up.
  • Use of internet or any devices are not allowed.
  • All the participants must bring their college or institution id card.
